The Barren Tree of Life

Acrylic on Canvas 31.5" × 31.5" Original · Priceless

"This painting portrays the Tree of Life without leaves or fruit, in the season of Winter. Bare branches stretch outwards to the heavens and the roots twist into the Earth below. The duality of day and night are divided by the tree's form. Above, the left side is ablaze in the colors of a setting sun and on the right side, stars streak across the midnight blue sky like a meteor shower."

A painting that holds both fire and night in a single frame — divided by the tree's silhouette. Seeing the Tree of Life in Winter allows the viewer to behold its full form, unobstructed by leaves or fruit.

About the Artist

Efren
Pardilla

Efren Pardilla is an artist born and raised in California, now living and working in Burbank, CA. He works as a TV & film editor — a dual life that deeply informs his art, where rhythm, contrast, and visual storytelling converge on canvas.

Each painting is 100% handmade by Efren Pardilla with high-end mixed medium acrylic paint on premium canvas. Originals are signed, dated, fingerprinted, and accompanied by a certificate of authenticity. High-quality prints of each work are also available.

His work spans the psychedelic and the philosophical: from the alien depths of the ocean rendered as another universe (Exoplanet Abyss), to the fractal geometry of sacred consciousness (Tetrahedron), to the political and spiritual divisions of our time. Great for a home, office, gallery, museum, gift, or as an investment.

Beyond the canvas, Efren is also the author of AquariuOS — an operating system-level creative project at aquariuos.com — further evidence of a mind that refuses to be confined to a single medium.
